Foodie Friend Finder Survey

I enjoy discovering weird, new, and non-american type resturants to try.

I enjoy the quality of the food and not the quantity of the food.

I enjoy having my food brought to me by a wait staff vs a buffett.

I enjoy meals that are simple to make more than foods that are hard to recreate.

I like to follow directions when recreating a recipe from another source.

I enjoy shopping for exotic ingredients so that I can create exotic recipes.

I prefer restaurants that uses local source ingredients over big chain restaurants.

I like when people enjoys and compliments my homemade recipe meals.

I enjoy dinning with a group of friends or family over eating by myself.

If the restuarant is very good based on reviews, I am okay with traveling over an hour to try it.
